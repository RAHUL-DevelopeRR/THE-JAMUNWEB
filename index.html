Overview

What is Java Plum?

The Java plum, commonly known as Jamun or Black plum, is a tropical tree native to the Indian subcontinent and Southeast Asia. This remarkable tree belongs to the Myrtaceae family and has been treasured for centuries for its medicinal properties, nutritious fruit, and durable timber.
